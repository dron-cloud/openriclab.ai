About

Research, mentorship, and real-world experimentation.

Dr. Dara Ron is an Assistant Research Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at NC State University. His work focuses on AI-native wireless systems, Open RAN, digital twins, and space networking.

He has published in IEEE journals, IEEE INFOCOM, and ACM venues, and serves as a reviewer and technical program committee member for leading communications and networking conferences.
